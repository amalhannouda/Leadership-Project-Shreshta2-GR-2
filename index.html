This website aims to help international students who have just moved to Kyoto and who need a mobile or Home internet contract. We have gathered in this website all information that we have deemed very useful according to our experiences.

We hope, this website will be useful to you and you will find information which will allow you to sign your internet contract with the best cost and in the shortest possible time.

Note:
This website will be helpful for those seeking long-term Internet contracts (typically one or more years).
