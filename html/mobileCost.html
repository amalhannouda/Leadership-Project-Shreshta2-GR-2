First of all, if you have WiFi at home and you will only use mobile data when outside the house, in this case 25GB/month should be more than enough. However, if you want big-data plans (26 GB or more per month). it's recommended to look into plans from docomo, AU, or SoftBank because they include 99% of the populated area of Japan. So you will be able to use the internet all over the country with no problem.

Secondly, if you are unsure of your Japanese, in case of problems while using your internet, such as work stoppage or unusually slow connection, it would be useful to have support in English. So, it's recommended to choose Sakura Mobile because it's one of the few companies to provide full support in English.

In sum:

  • If you need reliable English support 7 days a week, choose Sakura Mobile.
  • If you need unlimited data, choose SoftBank, Docomo, or AU.
  • If you have home internet and only need less than 25GB/month, you can choose the same company as home internet for your mobile. In this case, you can have a discount on your home internet fees.
