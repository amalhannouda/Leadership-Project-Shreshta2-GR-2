First of all, the fiber-optic port makes a noticeable difference in the monthly internet charges of around 750 yen. So we recommend checking it before renting an apartment.

According to the table above, if the apartment has a fiber optic port, the monthly Fee is around 4180 yen for all offers except for Nuro Hikari which is 5217 yen per month (the most expensive).

Secondly, you should be able to estimate how much data you will use per month. Depending on your data daily need, you can choose what offer will be convenient for you.

The table below gives an estimate of data needed per month depending on your connection needs:

In sum, the main factors that will allow you to choose the offer that best meets your needs are:

  • Fiber optic port
  • Estimated monthly data you will use
