In order to the opinions don't remain limited to the members of our group, we decided to ask some questions to people who are not students at the Kyoto College of Graduate Studies for Informatics concerning the subject of the Internet: the difficulties in signing an internet contract, the company used, and their satisfaction with the quality of service.

We conducted random interviews with people in Kamigamo, Imadegawa and Nijo districts.
