Conditions

House

To have more freedom in your choice, before renting your apartment check if it has Fiber Optic Port (hikari). If it doesn't have this Port, your choice of connection becomes very limited and more expensive.

  • Bank account

    • Having a bank account or a credit card is essential to sign an internet contract in Japan because invoices are paid by direct debit from your bank account. And besides, this is the first question that the agent will ask you before starting any action.

  • Residence card

    • Having a residence card valid for more than 4 months (for some companies more than 6 months) is essential even if you have a student card valid for 2 years. The company will ask you to renew your residence card. Then, you can start the process of signing the internet contract. So, if your residence card is valid for less than 6 months, it's better to renew it before starting the procedure.
